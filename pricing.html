Commercial Scale & Custom Facility Estimations

For corporate HQs, multi-tenant residential property portfolios, industrial kitchens, or medical clinic sanitization tracks, pricing parameters shift away from standard hourly blocks to precision sq. meter layout auditing metrics.

• Corporate Offices & Coworking Hubs

Calculated symmetrically on square-meter profiles. Standard workspace sanitation spans desk surface clearing, trash sorting, tech-screen dust extraction, high-traffic hallway buffing, and complete breakdown room restocks.

• Industrial Kitchen & Gastronomy Resets

Priced according to grease capture severity layers, heavy extraction hoods, and equipment footprint. Covers deep carbon scraping, under-counter machinery tracking, and commercial floor chemical flushes.

• Post-Construction & Renovation Stripping

Addresses fine crystalline plaster dust clouds, paint overspray splatters, heavy adhesive window seal tracking, and raw trim washdowns. Includes tactical industrial HEPA vacuum air filtration passes.

• Medical & Laboratory Disinfection Protocols

Strict custom tier modeling adhering to bio-safety requirements. Employs certified antimicrobial agents targeting point-of-contact hardware surface sweeps, waiting area rooms, and air systems care.
